Privacy Policy

1. What information NUadS collects and why

NUadS collects basic information from visitors to our website. We only require the minimum amount of personal information necessary from you, so as to ensure the basic functionality of NUadS. When you sign up or use NUadS, you give us certain information voluntarily, including your name, email address, phone number, profile photo etc.

2. Third Party Access

If you link your Facebook or Google account or accounts from other third party services to NUadS, we also get information from those accounts (such as your username, email address). The information we get from those services depends on your settings and thier privacy policies, so please ensure that you check what they are.

3. How we share the information we collect

We share information to provide the service to you, specifically, we require your personal information to filter out relevant surveys that match your profile information. We do not host advertising on NUadS and we do not sell your personal information.

4. How you can access and control the information we collect

We provide ways for your to access, alter or delete your profile information. This allows you to access and control the information we collect.
